STEP 1: LOGIN TO GODADDY
1. Go to: https://godaddy.com
2. Click 'Sign In' (top right)
3. Enter your email and password
4. You'll see your dashboard

STEP 2: FIND YOUR DOMAINS
1. Click 'My Products' 
2. Find 'Domains' section
3. You'll see: matrixfuture.dev, matrixfuture.net, matrixfuture.biz
4. We'll configure matrixfuture.dev FIRST

STEP 3: ACCESS DNS MANAGEMENT
1. Find 'matrixfuture.dev' in your list
2. Click the 3 dots (...) next to it
3. Select 'Manage DNS'
4. You'll see DNS Records table
